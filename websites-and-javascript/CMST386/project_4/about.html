About Us


Welcome to TomysNDavids. We are happy to have you here on our website!


Tomasa Diaz(Tomy) created TomysNDavids a few months after her first baby in 2018. Tomy became a stay at home mother and wanted to help her husband, so she decided to look for overflows of inventories from all the major retailers around her area. Within a few months, she opened a retail store on eBay, which to her surprise, began making, on average, seven sales per day. Within one year TomyNDavids had grown and in need of a standalone e-commerce site to better serve its customers. Our warehouse is located in Prince George's County, Maryland.
