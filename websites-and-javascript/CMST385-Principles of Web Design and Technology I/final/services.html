Services

Weddings

At Djmejia Photography we can photograph any type of event, but Weddings and Quinceañeras are what we shoot the most. Both of these types of events are somewhat similar in that both events generally have a church/ceremony, family and court portraits and a reception.

Quinceañeras

This is probably the event we enjoy shooting the most, Quinceañeras are so full of color, decorations and a lot of opportunities to get really beautiful photographs. Every time we shoot a Quinceañera the emotions are always around, and we try our best to capture it; everyone is so involved in this event, from uncles to siblings everyone plays an important role. If you choose Djmejia Photography to document your Quinceañera’s event, we can promise you that you are choosing the best photographers when it comes to amazing photographs, amazing packages, and amazing customer service. From the first time we meet on your free consultation, to the moment when we deliver your photographs our job is to become your photographer for life. We work with every budget big or small, we try our best to be competitive as well as reasonable, our prices are based on our experience, equipment and time spent, nothing more, nothing less.

Portraits

If you are looking for a portrait photographer for your next maternity , senior portraits or just any other important milestone in life, you came to the right place. Djmejia Photography is located in Capitol Heights MD, we are able to travel to any location in the DMV to capture your photograph that will last a lifetime. The best of all is that there is no session fee. all you pay is for your prints.
