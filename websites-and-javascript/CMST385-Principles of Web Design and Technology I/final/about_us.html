About Us

Djmejia Photography was founded on the August 2010 by David Jose Mejia hence the DJ in the business name. Before stablishing the company, David served as the lead photographer for Sears Portrait Studios in Bowie, Maryland for 4 years. During the course of those 4 years, David photographed over 2000 portrait sessions. This experience in photography and sales helped set the foundation for Djmejia Photography.
Djmejia Photography has been serving the DMV community for almost 10 years, photographing weddings, birthdays, quinceaneras, portraits, corporate events and now photo booths. We value customer service and the majority of our business consist of returning customers and refferals.
